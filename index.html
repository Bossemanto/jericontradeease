FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to the most common questions about our budgeting app
What currencies can I send to my supplier?
We support payments in Chinese Yuan (RMB) and US Dollars (USD) to Chinese suppliers.
How quickly are payments processed?
Most payments are processed and delivered to your supplier on the same day we receive your Naira transfer and supplier details.
How do I get the current exchange rate?
Simply request a rate and we'll provide you with the most competitive, real-time exchange rate for your transaction.
Are there any hidden fees?
No. All rates and fees are disclosed upfront before you confirm your transaction.
Who can use Jericon TradeEase?
Our service is designed for Nigerian businesses and importers paying Chinese suppliers.
How do I know my supplier has received the funds?
We send you a payment confirmation and receipt as soon as your supplier is credited.
Is my information secure?
Yes, we use secure channels and industry best practices to protect your data and transactions.
What details do I need to provide for a payment?
You'll need your supplier's Alipay, WeChat Pay, or USD bank details, and the amount to send.
